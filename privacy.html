Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 20, 2026

1. Who We Are

Shwe Pixel AI Studio ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes mobile and web games. This Privacy Policy applies to our apps, games, and related services.

2. Data We May Collect

Depending on the app and enabled features, we and our service providers may process:

  • Identifiers (for example, advertising ID, app instance ID, device identifiers).
  • Device and network data (for example, device model, OS version, IP-derived approximate region).
  • Usage and event data (for example, session activity, gameplay events, interaction data).
  • Diagnostics (for example, crash logs, performance and error reports).

3. How We Use Data

We use data to operate and improve our apps, maintain security, fix bugs, understand performance, prevent fraud, and (where enabled) deliver and measure ads.

4. Ads, Analytics, and Third-Party SDKs

Our apps may use third-party SDKs and services, including Google AdMob, Google Play services, and analytics/crash tools where applicable. These providers may process data according to their own privacy terms.

For AdMob-enabled apps, ad partners may process identifiers, device information, IP address, approximate location, and ad interaction data for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy, How AdMob Uses Data, and AdMob Publisher Policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers that support hosting, analytics, advertising, security, legal compliance, and app operations.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including legal, security, fraud prevention, and operational needs. Retention periods vary by data type and provider.

You may request deletion of data we control by contacting us. Some records may be retained where required by law, security, anti-fraud, or legitimate business needs.

7. Your Choices and Consent

Where required by law, consent choices for personalized ads are provided in-app through a supported consent flow. You may change ad/privacy choices through available in-app settings or your device settings where supported.

8. Children's Privacy

If an app is directed to children, we apply additional safeguards and use only compliant ad/SDK configurations. If you believe a child provided personal data improperly, contact us and we will review and take appropriate action.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. Google Play Data Safety Consistency

Our Google Play Data safety disclosures are intended to reflect the data handling of each app, including third-party SDK behavior. If there is any conflict for a specific app version, the app's latest Play Store disclosures and in-app disclosures should be considered controlling for that app version.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
